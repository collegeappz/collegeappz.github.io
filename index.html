About us

CollegeAppz is an early stage startup addressing the needs of 20 million U.S. and international high school students desiring to make smarter college choices. We're building a “TurboTax for College Admissions,” an one-stop shop for the entire college admissions and financial aid process.

Our beta went live in June 2013, and we’re gaining early traction and revenue. We're a very small team with big plans for the future. We’ve had write-ups in the Chronicle of Higher Education, TechCrunch, TechCocktail, and other news sources. We’ve partnered with the White House and U.S. Department of Education for a DataJam event focused on building the next generation of edtech tools. We're also partners with Pearson (#1 education company in the world) as their only college readiness provider.
